# Refocal Studio

A coming soon page for Refocal Studio — a small design practice focused on helping companies communicate complex ideas with clarity.

---

## About

Refocal Studio is built on a simple idea:

> Complex doesn’t have to feel complicated.

The goal is to work with teams to turn dense products, systems, and concepts into visuals people can understand quickly and intuitively.
